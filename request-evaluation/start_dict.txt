        Selection of two input parameters a oparand and a goal parameter (could all three be the same parameter)
        Oprands are + - * / etc und EQULAS AND OR XOR etc
    2. ALI - same as AL but with immediate instead of second parameter
    3. OSLM (Ordered Supplier List Modification):
        Modification of all entires (AL or ALI) in the supplier matrix that follow a seclection patter. Can only depend on top atttributes (those not in the supplier matrix and those in the supplier matri of a higher order)
    4. SRM (Supplier rank modification) - technically same as as OSLM but with rank being the fixed lowest ordered supplier list attribute
